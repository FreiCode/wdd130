Arizona

Arizona is a state in the western United States. It is well known for its hot and dry climate. Arizona has a wide elevation ranging from under 100ft to over 12,000ft above sea level. Arizona borders Mexico, California, Nevada, Utah, New Mexico, and shares a corner with Colorado.
