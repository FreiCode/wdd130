Purpose

The purpose of the rafting website will be to allow customers to see the services offered. The website should influence visitors to become customers. The site should include contact information, services, prices, reviews, location exc...

Audience

The target audience is one that is younger to middle age. They are set up well financially, enjoy the outdoors and are looking for their next adventure. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find information on toured rafting trips, prices, locations, testimonials, and contact information. What do they want? They want to see if this is an adventure that they would like to take and want information on how to book a trip. How will they usually access my site? Typically through laptops and mobile devices.
