Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the rafting website will be to allow custormers access to information such as services, prices, contact information, and custormer reviews. The goal will be to convince them to go on a rafting adventure with the company.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers are younger to middle age couples. They are well off financially and enjoy the outdoors. What do they want? The customers want to find their next adventure. They want to find information to make plans and book their next vacation, weekend or day trip. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They can expect to find contact information, images of the adventure, dates, prices, reviews, and what equipment is provided.
